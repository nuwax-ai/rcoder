## 异常恢复与资源清理

讨论异常断开时的恢复策略和资源清理机制。

```mermaid
flowchart TD
Start([会话异常断开]) --> CheckThread["检查AcpThread状态"]
CheckThread --> ThreadExists{"AcpThread存在?"}
ThreadExists --> |是| CleanupResources["清理相关资源"]
ThreadExists --> |否| End([处理完成])
CleanupResources --> RemoveFromSession["从会话管理器移除"]
RemoveFromSession --> NotifyObservers["通知观察者"]
NotifyObservers --> End
```
